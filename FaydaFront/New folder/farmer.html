About AgroLink

AgroLink is designed to bridge the gap between farmers and essential agricultural inputs like fertilizers and seeds. We aim to eliminate the issues caused by brokers who inflate prices and divert resources, ensuring you get what you need, when you need it, at fair market rates.

With AgroLink, you benefit from:

  • Transparent Access to Purchased Inputs: Clear and fair access without hidden charges.
  • Direct Link to Resource Providers: Connect directly with the government or designated distributors.
  • Elimination of Middlemen: No more exploitation by brokers.
  • Increased Trust: A transparent and direct process fosters confidence in the supply chain.

Your Fayda National ID is crucial for secure and verified transactions, protecting you from fraud and ensuring accurate data for effective resource distribution.

About AgroLink for Government

AgroLink serves as a critical tool for the government to manage and oversee the agricultural supply chain. It addresses the problem of intermediaries exploiting the system by providing direct access and real-time verification of transactions.

Key benefits for the government include:

  • Full Transparency in Input Sales: Clear oversight of where resources go and at what price.
  • Direct Connection for Resource Delivery: Efficient delivery to intended farmers.
  • Broker Elimination: Combating corruption and ensuring fair pricing.
  • Real-time Oversight: Tools for monitoring sales, tracking inventory, and identifying bottlenecks.
  • Enhanced Trust: Rebuilding farmer trust in the agricultural input supply chain.

The system leverages Fayda National ID to ensure only registered and eligible farmers collect inputs, providing accurate and trustworthy data.
